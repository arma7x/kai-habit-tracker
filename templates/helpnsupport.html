1) Habit Tracker only support daily counter
2) You can only click Relapse or Check-In once a day
3) Feel free to adjust your Target Day
4) What is consider as Positive:
* If you want to make good habits every day, pick Positive, eg; Learn English, Read Book, etc
* If your Target Day is 10, make sure to Check-In every day for 10 consecutive days starting from the Start Date to achieve a 100% Progress score
* Progress score is reset to 0 if you forgot to click Check-In everyday
* Do your best to keep Check-In every day to keep your Idle Days as low as possible
* High number of Idle Days is considered bad
* Low number of Check-In Counter is considered bad
5) What is consider as Negative:
* If you want to quit bad habits every day, pick Negative, eg; Quit Smoking, Quit Gambling, etc
* If your Target Day is 10, try your best not to Relapse between the Start Date to 10 days after the Start Date to achieve a 100% Progress score
* Progress score is reset to 0 if you click Relapse
* Try your best not to Relapse to keep your Restrain Days as high as posibble
* Low number of Restrain Days is considered bad
* High number of Relapse Counter is considered bad
